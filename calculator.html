4-bit Partial Calculator

Performs binary addition, subtraction, and multiplication between two 4-bit numbers.

I eventually would like to expand this project to include a division operation, 7-segment displays for user-friendliness, and larger inputs and output.

Materials

- 8x 1kΩ Resistor

- 1x Dip Switch 2 Position

- 2x Dip Switch 4 Position

- 4x SN74LS08N

- 4x SN74LS83N

- 1x SN74LS86N

- 4x SN74LS153N

- 8x LED
